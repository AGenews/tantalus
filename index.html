Welcome to Tantalus

Tantalus is a very simple program which easily runs on Linux, OSX and even Windows. It allows you to manually score e.g. freezing & digging behavior typically observed in behavioral experiments.

Why the name?

Tantalus (lat.) or TÃ¡ntalos (greek) is a Greek mythological figure most famous for his eternal punishment. I myself am a biologist and typically dislike experiments with negative affect to a certain degree - which is the main reason why I've picked that particular name.

What Tantalus is ...

Admittedly it is a very ugly replacement for an even uglier predecessor (written in 1986 for DOS). But my plans for May-July 2016 are to make a better version of it.

Tantalus is not ...

  • beautiful
  • bug-free
  • finished
  • 100% reliable

Will it change or even improve?

Hopefully yes! The rather - let's say - traditional appearance of Tkinter is my motivation to use Qt in the next release. Moreover there are a bunch of necessary modifications like automatic saving, user-defined keymap and names ...
